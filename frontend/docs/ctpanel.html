CT (Current Transformer) TAB PANEL

(CT) CURRENT TRANSFORMER MAKE:
Choices are displayed and can be selected. If the make is not in the selection then the relevant entry could be entered into the CT (Current Transformer) combobox textfield.

TYPE:
Choices are displayed and can be selected. If the make is not in the selection then the relevant entry could be entered into the Type combobox textfield.

CLASS:
Choices are displayed and can be selected. If the make is not in the selection then the relevant entry could be entered into the Class combobox textfield.

APPLICATION:
Choices are displayed and can be selected. If the application is not in the selection then the relevant entry could be entered into the Application combobox textfield.

RED SERIAL NUMBER:
Enter the serial number displayed on the relevant transformer.

 WHITE SERIAL NUMBER:
Enter the serial number displayed on the relevant transformer.

 BLUE SERIAL NUMBER:
Enter the serial number displayed on the relevant transformer.

RATIO:
Transformer ratios must be entered. The primary to secondary winding ratio of the current transformer would be entered into a dual textfield with the primary windings entered as an integer value into the first textfield and the secondary windings into the second textfield. . It's important that both values are entered for later calculations i.e. the percentage winding ratio error needs to be calculated.

VA RATING:
The VA rating will be the same for all three phases. It is important that all CT (Current Transformer) settings are correctly entered before testing.
